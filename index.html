Must Visit Destinations in the Philippines

The Philippines is an island country located in Southeast Asia. It is in the western part of the Pacific Ocean and is an archipelago. The Philippines consists of 7,641 islands. Seen below are some of the most visited places in the Philippines that you might want to check out too.

Boracay

Boracay is a famous tourist destination in our country. It is known for its beautiful powdered white sand and is located in the western region of Visayas found in the province of Aklan. You can go here for a peaceful rest, strolls on the beach, water activities, but you can also visit the many restaurants and bars they have at night.

Mount Mayon

Mount Mayon is an active stratovolcano in the southern part of Luzon. It is located in Albay, Bicol where many people go for vacation. Siargao

Siargao is a teardrop-shaped island found in the northeastern part of Mindanao. It is also known to be the Surfing Capital of the Philippines . You can go there during the dry season from March to September, or you can go during the Surfing season which starts in July and ends on November.
